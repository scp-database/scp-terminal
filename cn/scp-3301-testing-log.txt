The entity appears as an extremely large orangutan in stereotypical sailor's clothes, smoking a pipe, and holding a finished Rubik's Cube in their hand. A necklace is seen around their neck.

The entity speaks through American Sign Language, and speaks with the players for a short time. After 15 minutes, the entity reenters the escape pod. It begins to fly back into the spacecraft. Eventually, several large objects that resemble anchors are shot out of the spacecraft and begin to impale The Eel and The Great Leviathan, and begin to drag them up into the craft. Immediately afterwards, the game ends and Dr. Westrin and Junior Researcher Kim are rewarded the  win-condition.

脚注
1. 关于卡牌格式-在一个抽卡实例中,“Mr.Scary”被作为的描述。
2. 意指Maksur难度是未知的，并非被SCP官方正式承认的模式类别。
3. 由于这个事件之外没遇到过“大麻之壶”卡牌，所以尚不知道这是否是游戏功能。
4. 原先的无名卡片上的所有文字，除了类型-同伴，都被黑条覆盖了。
5. 尽管设置为Safe，游戏模式更像Neutralized，所有暴露的血液都被等量的苹果籽替代，啊，没有遇到功能性异常。
6. 虽然卡的颜色不同，但内容却相同。
7. 苹果籽卡牌的数值只适用于蓝色橙色或紫色卡。
